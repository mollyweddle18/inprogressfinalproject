3 Tips for Learning Chinese

  1. Learn 5-10 new vocab words daily

    • Make flashcards and review them frequently throughout the day
    • Do not just memorize the word, but memorize a sentence you can use it in

  2. Memorize words in the form of a story

    • Stories stick well in our heads, so memorizing a short story with your new vocab words will help them stick
    • If you are struggling to know what new words to learn, think of a life experience, say all that you can in Chinese, then learn the words that you did not know how to say

  3. Practice with native speakers

    • Practice, practice, practice!
    • You never know what you are saying wrong until you practice with a native!
